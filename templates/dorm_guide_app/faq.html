DormGuide FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is DormGuide?

DormGuide is a platform designed to help students find the best accommodations for their needs, offering user-friendly search and review features.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and filling in your details, such as name, email, and password.

How do I add properties to my favorites?

Once logged in, click the heart icon or "Add to Favorites" button on a property listing to save it to your profile.

Can I contact property owners through DormGuide?

Yes, you can contact property owners through the "Contact" button provided on each property listing page.

How do I remove a property from my favorites?

Go to your profile, locate the property under your favorite listings, and click the "Remove" button.
