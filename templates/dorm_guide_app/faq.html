FAQ

Here are some answers to our most frequently asked questions

How do I leave a review?

To leave a review, you'll need to be a student at one of our supported universities and register your account with your student email.
Once logged in, you'll be able to review any accommodation provided by your university.

Are your reviews trustworthy?

Yes.
All reviews on the site are made by verified students at each institution.
We will never edit reviews upon an institutions request.

I can't find my University on the site

Sorry about that!
If your University is not listed on our website you can contact us and request we add it.

Is Dorm Guide free to use?

Yes, Dorm Guide is free to use for everyone and always will be.
