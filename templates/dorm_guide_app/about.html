About Us

Welcome to Dorm Guide, the ultimate platform to help prospective and current university students make informed decisions about their accommodation options. Our mission is to provide authentic insights into university accommodations, making the process of selecting the perfect living space easier and more transparent.

What We Offer

  1. A comprehensive database of university accommodations across various institutions.
  2. Authentic reviews from previous and current students.
  3. Interactive maps displaying accommodation locations.
  4. Ability to sort accommodations by average rating.
  5. A user-friendly platform accessible to everyone, especially prospective students.

Prospective Students Can

  1. Browse through our extensive list of university accommodations.
  2. Access valuable information such as location, average rating, and student reviews.
  3. Make informed decisions about your future living arrangements without the need to create an account.

Current and Previous Students (with a valid .edu email address) Can

  1. Register and create an account to contribute to our growing community.
  2. Share their experiences by writing reviews of accommodations provided by their own university.
  3. Opt for anonymity when posting a review to ensure privacy.
  4. Upload a photo as part of their review to give prospective students a visual insight into the accommodation.

At Dorm Guide, We Are Committed To

  1. Ensuring that all users can easily register, edit, and delete accounts.
  2. Providing a secure platform for users to log in and out of their accounts.
  3. Maintaining the highest standards of content quality by only allowing current and previous students with valid .edu email addresses to post reviews.
  4. Ensuring privacy by allowing users to post reviews anonymously.
  5. Allowing users to upload photos as part of their reviews to provide a visual insight into accommodations.

Join Us!

Join Dorm Guide today and become part of our growing community, helping students make the best decision when it comes to choosing university accommodations!
